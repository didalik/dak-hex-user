To see how it works, use this Proof-of-Concept Demo - it runs on Stellar TESTNET and provides you with your own copy of the interactions outlined in the video. The demo output lets you analyze its results with Stellar Laboratory, making your review of the project's codebase a breeze. Run the demo!
